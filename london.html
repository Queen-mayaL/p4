London


The Historic and Cosmopolitan Capital:


London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a city steeped in history while thriving as a modern global hub for culture, finance, and politics.
Known for its iconic red double-decker buses, historic monuments, and vibrant neighborhoods, London offers something for everyone.
Here are its most famous landmarks:




Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament:

Big Ben, officially the Elizabeth Tower, is one of London's most iconic symbols.
It stands beside the Gothic-style Houses of Parliament on the banks of the River Thames.
The chimes of Big Ben are world-famous, and the complex is the center of British politics.



Buckingham Palace:

The official residence of the British monarch, Buckingham Palace is famous for its Changing of the Guard ceremony.
Its grand facade and surrounding gardens make it a must-see for visitors.
